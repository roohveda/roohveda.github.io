About RoohVeda

Bridging Ancient Wisdom with Modern Living

Our Mission

RoohVeda is a sanctuary for high-functioning individuals carrying invisible wounds—trauma, chronic stress, neurological and lifestyle suffering. We help people regulate their nervous system, re-inhabit their bodies, and reconnect with meaning.

"When the being heals, life becomes possible again." You are not broken. Your system is overwhelmed. It can be rewoven through integrative mind–body–soul medicine that blends ancient wisdom with modern science.

We serve people who appear okay from the outside yet are exhausted, anxious, numb, or struggling inside—offering structured support without requiring hospitalization or loss of daily functioning.

Core Values

Nervous System Healing

Regulate, restore, and recalibrate your body's natural ability to heal through evidence-informed practices.

Body-Mind-Soul Integration

Every person is a living ecosystem. We heal the whole being, not isolated symptoms.

Trauma-Sensitive Care

Safe, psychologically informed approaches that respect your unique journey and dignity.

Our Vision

RoohVeda envisions a world where the intersection of ancient wisdom and modern science creates pathways for true healing. We serve exhausted and anxious individuals seeking meaningful support—not through hospitalization, but through integrative, embodied practices that honor both neuroscience and spirit. We guide people from trauma survival into integrated, purposeful living.

About Dr. Roohi

Founder & Integrative Physician

Dr. Roohi (BNYS, MD, DGC) is an integrative physician with 9+ years of experience combining Ayurveda, neuroscience, somatic therapy, and spiritual wisdom. She founded RoohVeda to bridge the gap between trauma-informed care and holistic healing, creating a sanctuary where high-functioning individuals can safely regulate their nervous systems and reconnect with wholeness.

Dr. Roohi works with individuals, leads residential retreats, and collaborates with community organizations across India.
